```
[RECOMMENDATION]

→ DECISION: Trade quality vs quantity
→ Suggest: 08-16 UTC + opportunistic (best signals only)
```

## 💡 BEST PRACTICES

1. **Always walk-forward** — test on unseen data (not fit-forward)
2. **Include slippage** — assume -1% on entries, +1% on exits
3. **Account for fees** — Bybit maker/taker ~0.02%
4. **Consider regime changes** — 2020 ≠ 2022 ≠ 2024
5. **Compare vs benchmark** — BTC buy-hold, etc.
6. **Report uncertainty** — "Confident 90%" vs "Low confidence"
